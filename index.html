Who we are

Golden Company is a leading email delivery service provider, dedicated to delivering your parcels and packages with utmost care and efficiency. Our company has been in the business for over a decade and has built a reputation for creating pixel-perfect emails using HTML5, LESS, FreeMarker, and Handlebars.js.

With a history of proven Litmus and mobile device tests we strive to provide the best products to all our clients. Our team of highly trained professionals is committed to ensuring that your email is handled and delivered with the utmost care.

Hawkeye

Golden Company was responsible for designing and managing email campaign templates using Handlebars.js, Freemarker, and JSON/YAML files, and configuring global partials to generate personalized email content using Google user API data.

The company also ensured that the Figma creative was translated into pixel-perfect, fixed-width, mobile-responsive emails, and provided advice during creative meetings to determine feasibility. Additionally, we identified development hurdles during post-mortems to improve agency processes.
